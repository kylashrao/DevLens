Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

DevLens ("we," "us") operates devlens.dev. This page explains what data is and isn't collected when you use the site, including the JSON formatter and JWT decoder.

Your pasted data is never uploaded

Both the JSON formatter and the JWT decoder run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Any JSON, JWT, or other text you paste into either tool is processed locally on your device and is never sent to, stored on, or logged by our servers.

Cookies and advertising

This site may display advertising served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics tools to understand aggregate site traffic, such as page views and general geographic region. This data is anonymized and is not linked to anything you paste into either tool.

Third-party links

This site may link to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

Children's privacy

DevLens does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
